HotOS XX
The 20th Workshop on Hot Topics in Operating Systems

Location: Banff, Alberta, Canada.

Dates: May 14–16, 2025.

Welcome

Participants in the 20th Workshop on Hot Topics in Operating Systems will present and discuss new ideas in systems research and how technological advances and new applications are shaping our computational infrastructure. We welcome researchers and practitioners old and new.
